# The AI Courses Guide

**From free university lectures to certificate tracks - the best AI courses of 2026, sorted by goal and budget.** Almost every option lets you start for free; you usually only pay for graded work and a certificate.

## How to choose

Want pure, no-code AI literacy? Start with **AI For Everyone** or **Google AI Essentials**. Prefer hands-on practice? **DataCamp**, **Codecademy** and **Brilliant** make the lessons stick. Chasing one specific, cheap course? **Udemy** wins. Want a guided path plus community? **Zero To Mastery**. Creative or marketing work? **Skillshare**. Want a resume-ready credential? **edX** (top universities) and **LinkedIn Learning** (straight onto your profile).

## Best platforms for beginners

<img src="https://www.aimiracle.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/best-ai-courses-for-beginners.jpg" width="800" alt="Best AI courses for beginners">

Ten strong starting points, from no-code literacy to hands-on tracks:

- **Google AI Essentials** - *No-code AI at work.* Free: 7-day trial / free audit. From $49/mo. Built by Google and needs zero coding - the safest first step for anyone who just wants to use AI well at work. About 8-10 hours.
- **AI For Everyone (Andrew Ng)** - *AI literacy.* Free: Free audit. From $49 certificate. Taught by Andrew Ng in plain English - the big-picture AI literacy that makes every other course easier. About 6 hours, non-technical.
- **Brilliant** - *Interactive intuition.* Free: 7-day trial. From $12.49/mo. Short, interactive, gamified lessons on AI, neural networks and LLMs - about 15 minutes a day. Best if long lectures lose you.
- **DataCamp** - *Hands-on data & AI.* Free: Free Basic plan. From $28/mo. In-browser exercises with no setup - turns theory into hands-on ability fast. Covers ChatGPT, Python and ML basics.
- **Udemy** - *Cheap, specific courses.* Free: Free courses. From ~$14 / course on sale. A huge marketplace where you buy individual courses outright and own them for life - hard to beat for one specific topic on a budget.
- **Zero To Mastery** - *Structured career paths.* Free: Free tutorials. From $23/mo. Guided beginner-to-pro paths plus an active community and Discord - keeps beginners on track to job-ready.
- **Skillshare** - *Creative, practical AI.* Free: 1-month trial. From $14/mo. Short, project-based classes on AI for content, design and marketing - turns AI into projects you can apply the same week.
- **edX** - *University credibility.* Free: Free audit. From $50 certificate. AI courses from Harvard, MIT and IBM - free to audit, or paid for a credible verified certificate.
- **Codecademy** - *Learn AI by doing.* Free: Free intro courses. From $14.99/mo. Interactive, in-browser lessons that check your work as you go - great if you would rather build than watch.
- **LinkedIn Learning** - *Resume-ready certificates.* Free: 1-month trial. From $19.99/mo. Short, polished video courses; every certificate shows on the LinkedIn profile recruiters already check.

## University-grade: the best AI courses on edX

<img src="https://www.aimiracle.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/best-ai-courses-on-edx-certificate.jpg" width="800" alt="Best AI courses on edX">

edX, the nonprofit founded by Harvard and MIT, is where top universities put their AI courses online - and most can be audited for free.

- **CS50's Introduction to AI with Python** (HarvardX, Beginner, Free to audit) - The famous free-to-audit on-ramp: build the ideas behind modern AI yourself in Python - search, knowledge, optimisation, machine learning, neural networks and NLP. ~7 weeks.
- **Generative AI for Everyone** (IBM, Beginner, Free to audit) - A 5-course Professional Certificate that demystifies ChatGPT-style tools - what LLMs are, prompt engineering, real use cases and ethics. No coding required.
- **AI for Business Users** (Microsoft, Intermediate, Free to audit) - A 7-course Professional Certificate built for results, not code - put Copilot and AI to work across marketing, operations and admin with no-code workflows.
- **Data Science: Machine Learning** (HarvardX, Intermediate, Free to audit) - A rigorous, hands-on intro to how machine learning really works - training/test data, cross-validation, KNN, regression and trees - by building a movie-recommendation system in R.
- **Python for Data Science & Deep Learning** (HarvardX, Intermediate, Free to audit) - A 3-course Professional Certificate taking you from Python basics to building neural networks - a strong technical track for aspiring practitioners.
- **Advanced: Generative AI for Developers** (Google Cloud, Advanced, Free to audit) - A 9-course Professional Certificate for engineers shipping generative-AI apps - LLMs, embeddings, retrieval, tuning and Google Cloud's Vertex AI tooling.
- **AI: Implications for Business Strategy** (MIT Sloan, Executive, Paid program) - A premium MIT Sloan Executive Education program - the management view of AI: spotting opportunities, building an AI-ready organisation, governance. Verified MIT certificate.

## Best AI courses on Udemy

<img src="https://www.aimiracle.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/best-ai-courses-on-udemy.jpg" width="800" alt="Best AI courses on Udemy">

Top-rated bestsellers, hands-on, and usually cheap on sale - the list prices are almost never what you pay.

- **The Complete AI Guide** (Generative AI & tools, 41.5h, $22.99 - was $99.99) - The no-code on-ramp: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and dozens of tools plus AI agents, applied to real everyday tasks. Full lifetime access and certificate.
- **A.I. & Machine Learning Bootcamp (ZTM)** (Full ML + data science, 43.5h, $19.99 - was $109.99) - Andrei Neagoie's single-course route from total beginner to hireable - data science, ML, neural networks and TensorFlow through real projects.
- **The AI Engineer Course 2026** (Building AI apps & LLMs, 29.5h, $13.99 - was $49.99) - The complete AI-engineer toolbox: Python for NLP, large language models, LangChain and Hugging Face applied to real business cases.
- **Artificial Intelligence A-Z 2026** (Agentic AI & RL, 17h, $22.99 - was $119.99) - A build-it-yourself tour of modern AI - prompt engineering, generative AI, agentic AI and reinforcement learning across 12 hands-on AI projects.
- **Machine Learning A-Z** (ML foundations (Python & R), 49h, $27.99 - was $149.99) - The best-selling ML course - regression, classification, clustering and deep learning, step by step, with ready-made Python & R code templates.
- **The Complete Prompt Engineering Bootcamp** (Prompt engineering, 22.5h, $14.99 - was $79.99) - Written by the authors of an O'Reilly book on prompting - GPT-5, Midjourney and 20+ real projects. The biggest payoff for a single weekend.
- **Ultimate AWS Certified AI Practitioner** (An AI certification, 10h, $15.99 - was $49.99) - Stephane Maarek's prep for the official AWS AIF-C01 exam - AI/ML/generative-AI on AWS (Bedrock, SageMaker, Amazon Q) plus a full practice exam.
